Lessons Learned

The process at the very beginning is so crucial to getting the logo right. So much information comes out from the simple discussions you have. Trying to capture the personality and potential is so challenging. I enjoy the process with pretty much every identity I've worked on. Many of them have been for personal projects or family related, I've been lucky that my family members are amazing clients.
